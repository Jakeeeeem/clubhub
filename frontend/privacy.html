Privacy Policy

At ClubHub, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or make a payment.

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth.
  • Payment Information: Transaction history (processed securely via Stripe).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including:

  • Processing transactions and sending related information.
  • Managing your account and providing customer support.
  • Sending technical notices, updates, and security alerts.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Club Administrators: If you join a club, they will have access to your profile data.
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors (like payment processors) who need access to perform services.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@clubhubsports.net.
